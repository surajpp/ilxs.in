About Us

ILXS is formed with a vision to provide solutions for digital documentation. We focus on continual development for documentation products that will be compatible with new as well as old hardware systems. We are a team of engineers with experience in the field of technical documentation, software development, and animations. Together, we have developed products that, we believe, will significantly help the defence and civilian sectors.



ILXS offers solutions like Interactive Electronic Technical Manuals (IETM), Computer Based Training (CBT), S1000D (authoring and publishing), Documentation, Industrial Photography and Web App Development. These products have proved to reduce the training and maintenance time for all types of mechatronic systems. Our aim is to further enhance the user experience while working with technical literature. We strive to deliver the highest quality output maintaining user-friendly approach.



All our products comply with recognized documentation standards such as JSS, JSG, MIL and S1000D. Joining the Atmanirbhar pledge of Indian Government, we would like to participate to make India a self-reliant nation.
