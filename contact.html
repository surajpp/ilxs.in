FAQ

1. What does ILXS Pvt. Ltd. offer?

Ans. ILXS offer products in the category of Interactive Electronic Technical Manual (IETM), S1000D, CBT and Record Management System(RMS). We are capable to developing software solutions to any customer requirements in the field of documentation.


2. What is IETM?

Ans. Interactive Electronic Technical Manual - IETM is categorised into 5 classes depending on the requirement of features. For more details visit products section.


3. What is CBT?

Ans. Computer Based Training - all the product description, operation and training are available in application in the form of interactive animated voice-over videos.


4. What is the level of modification available in IETM at customer end?

Ans. Content Modification using word editor and XML or HTML editor which ever form opted with the IETM package. Add, edit, delete options are available for Pages, Links, Images and Videos. Users can manual define n number of hotspot for image.


5. What is login based content access?

Ans. All the products of ILXS feature credential based access to the content.


6. What is the limit for node creation in Index?

Ans. The only thing which limits the amount of data is, the amount of system's capability to process database at normal operation.


7. What is OS requirement for ILXS products?

Ans. ILXS provides a complete backward compatibility to the decade old hardware, while maintaining latest technology in application development.


8. What is the level of ease provided in installation?

Ans. All ILXS products are provided in 4 to 8 installation steps, we believe in keeping a simple installation procedure.
